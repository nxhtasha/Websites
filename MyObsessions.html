The Flash

I recentely finished the last episode of The Flash. This CW show is about a teenage boy named Barry Allen who got struck by lightning and hit by dark matter which gave him superpowers. Now, with the help of his friends at S.T.A.R Labs, he fights crime and finds other metahumans like himself. A metahuman is a person who got hit with dark matter, like Barry, and became a supervillian, instead of a superhero(like Barry).
